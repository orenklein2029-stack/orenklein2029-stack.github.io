Stocks

A stock is a small piece of ownership in a company. When you buy a stock, you become a part-owner of that business. If the company does well, your stock goes up in value.

People invest in stocks because they can grow much faster than leaving money in a savings account. However, stocks can also go down in value, so there is always some risk involved.

Famous Stocks

  • Apple (AAPL)
  • Google (GOOGL)
  • Amazon (AMZN)
  • Tesla (TSLA)
