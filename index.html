Intro to Finance

Finance is the study of how people and businesses manage money. It covers earning, spending, saving, and investing to reach financial goals.

Investing means putting your money into assets like stocks or bonds with the hope that it will grow over time. Starting early gives your money more time to grow through compound interest.

  • Investing in stocks and bonds
  • Creating and sticking to a budget
