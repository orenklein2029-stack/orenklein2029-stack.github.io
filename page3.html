Budgeting

A budget is a plan for how you spend your money. It helps you make sure you have enough for things you need before spending on things you want.

A popular rule is the 50/30/20 rule — spend 50% on needs, 30% on wants, and save 20%.

Budgeting Tips

  • Track every purchase you make
  • Set a savings goal each month
  • Avoid impulse buying
  • Review your budget weekly
